Pet Portraits:

I have been an Artist for many years and have always found inspiration from my surroundings. For instance, I drew festival posters and painted Sealife when I lived on the coast in Florida. About six years ago I relocated to Atlanta and I decided to concentrate on commissioned hand drawn pet portraits because it allowed me to combine two of my favorite things: animals and art.

The three tools I highly recommend for a successful pet portrait would be: a good and clear reference photo, Prismacolor Colored Pencils and Strathmore Bristol Paper.
